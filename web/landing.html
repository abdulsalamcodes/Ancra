What you get

Everything to power payment collection.

Ancra wraps Nomba's banking rails into a clean API designed for developer teams.

Dedicated virtual accounts

Each customer gets their own unique NGN bank account number. Inbound payments route automatically to the correct ledger — no shared accounts, no manual matching.

Real-time webhooks

HMAC-SHA256 signed events delivered the moment a payment lands. Automatic exponential-backoff retry up to 5 attempts. Zero double-processing with built-in idempotency.

Double-entry reconciliation

Every naira is double-posted across customer and system ledgers. Automated sweeps cross-reference your ledger against Nomba's records and surface discrepancies immediately.

Paginated statements

Pull account statements with accurate running balances per entry — even across paginated pages. Ready to generate PDF statements for your customers.

Outbound transfers

Send funds from any virtual account to any Nigerian bank. Account name lookup built in. Ledger debits and automatic reversals on failure keep balances consistent.

KYC tier management

Customers carry Tier 1, 2, or 3 KYC status. Pass BVN and NIN at account provisioning time — Nomba enforces the right limits per tier automatically.

How it works

From API call to credited ledger.

Four steps. One clean flow. Your customers never wait for manual matching.

1
Provision a virtual account
POST /accounts with your customer's details. Get back a unique NGN bank account number backed by Nomba.
2
Share the account number
Display the account number to your customer. Any NIP transfer to that number triggers the payment flow.
3
Receive a signed webhook
Ancra verifies the HMAC signature, matches the account, and credits the customer's ledger — automatically.
4
Query balance anytime
GET /accounts/{id}/balance returns the current kobo balance. Pull statements for downloadable reports.
