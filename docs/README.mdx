Welcome to Ancra — the Dedicated Virtual Account (DVA) infrastructure API for Nigerian bank payments.

Ancra lets you provision virtual Nigerian bank account numbers for your customers, receive payments via NIP transfers, credit wallets automatically, and initiate outbound transfers — all through a single REST API.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provision an account">
    Create a virtual Nigerian bank account number for each of your customers. Each account is backed by Nomba and ready to receive NIP transfers immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer sends payment">
    Your customer transfers money to their unique virtual account number from any Nigerian bank account — mobile app, USSD, internet banking, or POS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ancra credits the ledger">
    Nomba sends a `payment_success` webhook. Ancra verifies the HMAC signature, resolves the virtual account, and posts a double-entry credit to the ledger atomically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your webhook fires">
    Ancra enqueues an outbound webhook delivery to your endpoint so your application can react in real-time — update order status, send notifications, or trigger workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and transfer">
    Check balances and transaction history through the ledger API. Initiate outbound bank transfers to any Nigerian account when needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

- **Instant virtual accounts** — provision Nigerian NUBAN account numbers on demand
- **Automatic ledger** — every transaction is double-entry, balances are always derived
- **Real-time webhooks** — get notified the moment a payment lands
- **Idempotent by design** — duplicate events are safely ignored
- **KYC tiers** — align with CBN's tiered framework (Tiers 1–3)
- **BYOK Nomba** — each organisation can bring their own Nomba credentials
- **Reconciliation** — automatic sweep compares your ledger to Nomba's wallet
